This page describes some of my favorite development economics readings, especially that are related to Economics 24.

Deep Determinants (light reading)
  • The Elusive Quest for Growth, Bill Easterly
  • Poor Economics, Banerjee and Duflo
  • The Wealth and Poverty of Nations, David Landes
Deep Determinants (heavy reading)
  • Seeing Like a State, James Scott
  • The Origins of Political Order, and Political Order and Political Decay, Francis Fukuyama
  • The Narrow Corridor, Acemoglu and Robinson
Order, Violence, and Corruption
  • The Sicilian Mafia, Diego Gambetta
  • The Social Order of the Underworld, David Skarbek
  • Gomorrah, Roberto Saviano
Ethnographic / Journalistic
  • Poor People, William Vollmann
  • Mountains Beyond Mountains, Tracy Kidder
  • Beyond the Beautiful Forevers, Katherine Boo
  • In the Footsteps of Mr. Kurtz, Michela Wrong
Fiction
  • Mister Taylor, Augusto Monterroso (a short story)
  • Disgrace, J. M. Coetzee
  • Waiting for the Barbarians, J. M. Coetzee
  • Things Fall Apart, Chinua Achebe
  • The White Tiger, Aravind Adiga
  • Under the Red Flag, Ha Jin
  • Every Day is for the Thief, Teju Cole
U.S. Political Meltdown
  • The Dictator's Handbook, de Mesquita and Smith
  • How Democracies Die, Ziblatt and Levitsky
  • How to Rig and Election, Cheeseman and Klaas
  • The Revolt of the Elites, Christopher Lasch
No Category
  • Stubborn Attachments, Tyler Cowen
  • Barbarian Days, William Finnegan
  • The Man Who Mistook His Wife for a Hat, Oliver Sacks
  • Among the Thugs, Bill Buford
